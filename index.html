Why do you need secure passwords?

In today's digital age, secure passwords are your first line of defense against identity theft and unauthorized access to your accounts. A strong password can make the difference between keeping your information safe or becoming a victim of a cyberattack.

Characteristics of a secure password

  • Minimum length: At least 12 characters to resist brute-force attacks.
  • Complexity: Combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Uniqueness: Different for each account to avoid compromising multiple services.
  • Randomness: No predictable patterns or personal information.

Tips for managing your passwords

  1. Use a reliable password manager to store your credentials securely.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication whenever available.
  3. Change your passwords regularly, especially after a security breach.
  4. Do not share your passwords or reuse them across multiple services.
